Tea Bread Recipe

Description

This delicious treat that is perfect for coffee and tea.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 2 TBSP Sugar or honey or agave or maple syrup
  • salt 1/8tsp ( note this is less than 1/4tsp)
  • 1/4tsp Nut Meg
  • Softened Butter 1/4cup (real butter)
  • 2 TBSP Flaxseed
  • 0.75 cup (less than 1 cup) Plant milk
  • half Tsp Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Sift out the flour, sugar, and baking powder in a mixing bowl. This helps to eliminate and prevent lumps that would weigh down the batter.
  2. To this, add salt and nutmeg then mix it very well with a fork.
  3. Add refrigerated cold or softened butter and rub it in the mixture using your fingertips. Continue until the mixture resembles fine bread crumbs.
  4. In a separate bowl, pour in Plant milk, vanilla extract, and flaxseed liquid. Beat the mixture with a fork to get a nice even color.
  5. Add the beat liquid to the flour mixture little by little while stirring gently with a spoon. Do not mix it completely.
  6. Put the batter in a greased baking pan and even it out by shaking it gently..
  7. Bake for 30 minutes at 350D.
  8. Serve it hot
